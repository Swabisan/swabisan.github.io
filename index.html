Rapid.io Hackathon

Summer 2017

This was at a hackathon hosted by Rapid.io, they're a database company that was showcasing their new realtime database, which at the time was in closed beta. I had a great time, worked with great people. The project that we were working on was a dashboard that would be able to sort and reapportion objects based on changes in their values. After a multitude of obsticles and pitfalls we were able to get a modest tech demo working, however 24 hour crunch was much more draining than anticipated, it might be a little while until I go to another hackathon!
